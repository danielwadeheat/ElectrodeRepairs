About Electrode Repairs

We're your local electronics repair shop in Newcastle, OK — built on trust, quality, and community.

Our Story

Electrode Repairs was founded right here in Newcastle, Oklahoma — a locally owned shop built from a passion for technology and a commitment to helping the community keep their devices running.

We started because we knew that too many people were replacing devices that could easily be repaired. The cost of new electronics adds up fast, and we wanted to offer our neighbors a smarter, more affordable alternative.

From our first smartphone screen repair to the hundreds of devices we fix every month, our mission has always stayed the same: give every customer honest service, quality repairs, and a fair price.

We Believe in the Right to Repair

Electronics are expensive, and every device that gets repaired instead of replaced is a win — for your wallet and for the environment. We support the Right to Repair movement, which advocates for consumers' ability to have their devices serviced by local shops rather than being forced into costly manufacturer replacements.

When you bring your device to us, you're making a choice that matters. You're extending the life of that device, reducing electronic waste, and keeping money in your local community. We're proud to be that option for Newcastle and the surrounding area.
