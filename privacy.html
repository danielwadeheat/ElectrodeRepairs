Privacy Pledge

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: 2024

Electrode Repairs ("we," "us," or "our"), located at 1025 N Main St, Newcastle, OK 73065, is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Pledge explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with us:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and mailing address when you submit a form, request a quote, or contact us.
  • Device Information: Details about your device (make, model, issue) when you request a repair quote or sell/buy a device.
  • Communication Records: Records of calls, emails, or messages you send us.
  • Website Usage Data: Standard web analytics data such as pages visited, time on site, and browser type — collected anonymously.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide repair quotes
  • Process device repairs, purchases, and sales
  • Contact you about your service or transaction
  • Send appointment reminders or follow-up communications
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

3. Text Message (SMS/MMS) Communications

By providing your phone number and opting in, you may receive SMS or MMS messages from Electrode Repairs related to your repair status, appointment reminders, or promotional offers. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message. For help, reply HELP or contact us at Info@electroderepairs.com.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in our operations (such as financing partners like Acima), under appropriate confidentiality agreements
  • As required by law or to respond to legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Electrode Repairs, our customers, or others

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to protect your own information as well.

6. Your Devices

When you bring a device in for repair, we handle it with care and respect your privacy. We do not access personal data on your device unless it is necessary to perform the requested repair and you give us permission to do so.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Pledge from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
